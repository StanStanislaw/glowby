4. App Tracking Transparency

Glowby uses Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework. Before any tracking occurs, you will be asked for explicit permission. If you decline, only non-personalized, contextual ads will be shown. You can change this permission at any time in your device's Settings → Privacy → Tracking.

5. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Important: Glowby is rated 4+ and safe for all ages. We do NOT knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

For users under 13:

  • We do not collect, use, or disclose personal information
  • Personalized advertising is disabled
  • Only contextual (non-targeted) ads are shown
  • No behavioral tracking occurs

Parents may contact us at Loading... to review, delete, or refuse further collection of their child's information.
