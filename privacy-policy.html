3. Children's Privacy

Important: Glowby is rated 4+ and is suitable for all ages. We take children's privacy very seriously.

3.1 COPPA Compliance (United States)

The Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) applies to children under 13. Glowby:

  • Does NOT knowingly collect personal information from children under 13
  • Uses age-gating to identify users under 13
  • For users identified as under 13, we disable personalized advertising and limit data collection
  • Does NOT require personal information to play the game

If you believe we have inadvertently collected data from a child under 13, please contact us immediately and we will delete it.

3.2 GDPR-K (EU/EEA)

For users in the EU/EEA under the age of 16 (or the applicable age in your country: 13-16 depending on member state), we:

  • Only serve contextual (non-personalized) advertisements
  • Do not collect advertising identifiers
  • Require parental consent for any data processing beyond core gameplay

3.3 Other Jurisdictions
