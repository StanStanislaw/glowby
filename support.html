How to Play

Basic Rules

  1. Drop Tiles — Tap a column to drop the current tile
  2. Merge Numbers — When identical tiles touch, they combine (2+2=4, 4+4=8, etc.)
  3. Beat the Timer — Drop your tile before time runs out, or the game ends!
  4. Avoid the Asteroid Field! — If any tile lands in the danger zone at the bottom, the game ends instantly!
  5. Reach 2048 — Keep merging to reach the golden 2048 tile and beyond!

Tips for High Scores

  • Build combos — Chain multiple merges for bonus points!
  • Watch the asteroid field — If tiles get too high, you're in danger!
  • Plan ahead — Look at your next tile and position strategically
  • Stay calm — The timer gets faster as you progress. Stay focused!

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my purchases?

Go to Settings → Shop → Restore Purchases. This will recover any previously purchased items on your Apple ID.

Why did I lose my progress?

Progress is stored locally on your device. If you reinstalled the app or switched devices, your progress may not transfer. For leaderboard scores, make sure you're signed in with the same username.

How do I change my username?

Go to Settings and tap the edit button next to your username. Note: Inappropriate names may be reset.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings → scroll to the bottom → tap "Delete Account & Data". This will permanently remove your leaderboard entries and local data.

Can I play offline?

Yes! The core game works offline. However, leaderboards and cloud saves require an internet connection.

How do I disable ads?

Purchase the "Free Continues" pack in the Shop to remove interstitial ads and get unlimited continues.

The game is running slowly. What can I do?

Try enabling "Smooth Mode" in Settings → Performance. This reduces visual effects for better performance on older devices.

Report a Bug

Found a bug? Please email us with:

  • Your device model (e.g., iPhone 14)
  • iOS version (e.g., iOS 17.2)
  • App version (shown in Settings)
  • Steps to reproduce the issue
  • Screenshots if possible

Tip: Check the App Store for updates — we regularly fix bugs and add improvements!

Refund Requests

All in-app purchases are processed by Apple. To request a refund:

  1. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com
  2. Sign in with your Apple ID
  3. Find your Glowby purchase
  4. Select "Request a refund"

Note: Refunds are at Apple's discretion. Coins spent before a refund cannot be recovered.
