‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is Glowby free to play?

Yes! Glowby is completely free to download and play. There are optional in-app purchases for coins and skins, but they are not required to enjoy the full game.

Q: How do I earn coins?

You earn coins by playing the game - the higher your score, the more coins you earn! You can also watch optional rewarded video ads for bonus coins.

Q: How do leaderboards work?

Your best scores are automatically submitted to our global leaderboards. You can set a display name in the game settings to show on the leaderboard. Internet connection is required for leaderboards.

Q: Can I play offline?

Yes! The core game works offline. However, leaderboards and rewarded ads require an internet connection.

Q: How do I change my display name?

Go to Settings (gear icon) in the game and tap on "Change Name". Your name will update on the leaderboard after your next score submission.

Q: What skins are available?

Glowby has many skins to unlock! Some can be purchased with coins, others are earned by reaching milestones (like achieving 2048 or 4096), and special skins are available through various means.

Q: My progress was lost. Can I recover it?

Game progress is stored locally on your device. If you uninstall the app or reset your device, progress cannot be recovered. We recommend not deleting the app if you want to keep your progress.

Q: How do I delete my data?

Go to Settings in the game and tap "Delete Account". This will remove your leaderboard entries and reset all local data. This action cannot be undone.

Q: Can I get a refund for a purchase?

Refunds are handled by Apple through the App Store. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com to request a refund from Apple.

Q: How do I disable personalized ads?

You can control ad personalization in your device settings: Settings ‚Üí Privacy ‚Üí Tracking ‚Üí disable for Glowby. You can also opt out when the consent prompt appears on first launch.
