How to Play

Basic Rules

  1. Drop Tiles — Tap a column to drop the current tile
  2. Merge Numbers — When identical tiles touch, they combine (2+2=4, 4+4=8, etc.)
  3. Beat the Timer — Drop your tile before time runs out, or the game ends!
  4. Avoid the Asteroid Belt! — If any tile lands in the Asteroid Belt at the top, the game ends instantly!
  5. Reach 2048 — Keep merging to reach the golden 2048 tile and beyond!

Tips for High Scores

  • Build combos — Chain multiple merges for bonus points!
  • Watch the Asteroid Belt — If tiles get too high, you're in danger!
  • Plan ahead — Look at your next tile and position strategically
  • Stay calm — The timer gets faster as you progress. Stay focused!

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my purchases?

Go to Shop → Restore Purchases. This will recover any previously purchased items on your Apple ID.

Why did I lose my progress?

Progress is stored locally on your device and synced via iCloud if enabled. If you reinstalled the app or switched devices, make sure you're signed into the same Apple ID so iCloud can restore your data. For leaderboard scores, ensure you're signed into Game Center.

How do I change my username?

Go to Settings and tap the edit button next to your username. Note: Inappropriate names may be reset.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings → scroll to the bottom → tap "Delete Account & Data". This will permanently remove your leaderboard entries and local data.

Can I play offline?

Yes! The core game works fully offline. Leaderboards, cloud saves, and rewarded video ads require an internet connection.

How do I remove ads?

Purchase the "Free Continue Pack" in the Shop to remove ads and get unlimited continues. All ads in Glowby are optional rewarded videos — they are never forced or shown mid-gameplay.

The game is running slowly. What can I do?

Try enabling "Smooth Mode" in Settings → Performance. This reduces visual effects for better performance on older devices.

How does iCloud sync work?

When signed into iCloud, your game progress, unlocked skins, achievements, and settings sync automatically across all your Apple devices. Make sure iCloud is enabled in your device Settings.

Report a Bug

Found a bug? Please email us with:

  • Your device model (e.g., iPhone 15, iPad Air)
  • iOS version (e.g., iOS 18.3)
  • App version (shown in Settings)
  • Steps to reproduce the issue
  • Screenshots if possible

Tip: Check the App Store for updates — we regularly fix bugs and add improvements!

Refund Requests

All in-app purchases are processed by Apple. To request a refund:

  1. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com
  2. Sign in with your Apple ID
  3. Find your Glowby purchase
  4. Select "Request a refund"

Note: Refunds are at Apple's discretion. Coins spent before a refund cannot be recovered.
